## Notes

- Multiple contributors per poem are allowed; list in order of submission date.
- Contribution types may be combined, e.g. `E, A, R` means the contributor handled extraction, AI processing, and human review.
- Notes are optional but encouraged for significant corrections, e.g. "Fixed 2 invalid anchors", "Added 3 missing 音韵 entries".
- If a contributor performed content review only (without format checking), note "content review only" in the Notes column.
